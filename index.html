Kedarnath

Kedarnath, a sacred Hindu pilgrimage site in Uttarakhand, is dedicated to Lord Shiva. Its temple, believed to be built by the Pandavas, dates back to the 8th century.

Ram Mandir

The Ram Mandir in Ayodhya has a complex history. It is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama, and a temple was demolished by Mughal ruler Babur in the 16th century to build a mosque. After decades of disputes, a new temple is now under construction on the site.

Jagannath Puri

Jagannath Puri, a holy city in Odisha, is home to the famous Jagannath Temple, dedicated to Lord Jagannath, an avatar of Lord Vishnu. The temple's history dates back to the 12th century.

Varanasi

Varanasi, one of the world's oldest cities, has been a spiritual hub for over 2,500 years, and known for its rich cultural and historical significance.

Prayag

Prayag, now Prayagraj, is an ancient city known for its sacred confluence of rivers, significant in Hindu mythology and pilgrimage.

Rishikesh

Rishikesh, a holy city in Uttarakhand, has a rich history dating back to ancient times. It is mentioned in Hindu scriptures and is believed to be the site where Lord Rama did penance after killing Ravana

Rameswaram

Rameswaram, a sacred site in Tamil Nadu, is believed to be where Lord Rama worshipped Shiva after defeating Ravana. The Ramanathaswamy Temple, dating back to ancient times, attracts pilgrims.

Siddhivinayak

The Siddhivinayak Temple in Mumbai dates back to 1801 when it was built by Laxman Vithu and Deubai Patil. It is one of the most popular Hindu temples in the city, dedicated to Lord Ganesha.Agra
